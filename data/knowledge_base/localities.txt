Popular Localities Guide - Bangalore

Whitefield:
- IT hub with major tech parks (ITPL, Prestige Tech Park)
- Price range: 50-120 Lakhs for 2-3 BHK apartments
- Good connectivity via metro (upcoming Purple Line extension)
- Top projects: Prestige Shantiniketan, Brigade Lakefront, Sobha Dream Acres
- Best for: IT professionals, families

Sarjapur Road:
- Fast-growing residential corridor near IT hubs
- Price range: 40-100 Lakhs for 2-3 BHK apartments
- Multiple schools, hospitals, and shopping malls
- Top projects: Prestige City, Sobha Dream Acres, Godrej Splendour
- Best for: Young families, first-time buyers

Electronic City:
- Affordable IT hub in South Bangalore
- Price range: 30-70 Lakhs for 2-3 BHK apartments
- Well-connected via NICE Ring Road and Elevated Expressway
- Top projects: Puravankara Zenium, Concorde Manhattans
- Best for: Budget-conscious IT professionals

Hebbal:
- Premium North Bangalore locality
- Price range: 80-200 Lakhs for 2-4 BHK apartments
- Close to airport, Manyata Tech Park
- Top projects: Embassy Springs, Brigade Orchards, Tata Carnatica
- Best for: Senior professionals, NRIs

Devanahalli / North Bangalore:
- Emerging growth corridor near airport
- Price range: 40-90 Lakhs for 2-3 BHK apartments
- Upcoming metro connectivity and infrastructure
- Top projects: Tata Swaram, Brigade El Dorado, Godrej Ananda
- Best for: Long-term investors, airport employees
