Frequently Asked Questions - Pricing and Payments

Q: What is the minimum budget to buy a property?
A: We have 1BHK apartments starting from 25 Lakhs in locations like Electronic City, Hosur Road, and outskirts of Bangalore.

Q: Do you help with home loans?
A: Yes! We have tie-ups with all major banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis Bank. We help with documentation and can get you pre-approved before site visits.

Q: What are the additional costs apart from the property price?
A: Typically you should budget for:
- Registration charges: 5-6% of property value
- GST: 5% for under-construction, 0% for ready-to-move
- Maintenance deposit: 1-2 years advance
- Legal and documentation charges: Rs 10,000 - 25,000

Q: Can I pay in installments?
A: For under-construction properties, most builders offer construction-linked payment plans. Some also offer subvention schemes where you pay only 10-20% upfront.

Q: Do you charge brokerage?
A: Our brokerage is 1-2% of the property value, which is standard in the industry. For some builder partnerships, the brokerage is covered by the builder and is free for the buyer.

Q: What documents do I need?
A: For property purchase, you will need:
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Address proof
- Income proof (salary slips or ITR)
- Bank statements (6 months)
- Passport size photos
